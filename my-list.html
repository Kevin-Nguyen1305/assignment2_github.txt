✨ Top 3 Ways to Boost Your Creativity ✨

1. Start Journaling

Writing daily helps you clear your mind and find inspiration in unexpected thoughts. Try morning pages or sketch journaling!

2. Go for a Walk

Stepping away from screens and into nature can recharge your imagination. Bring a friend or enjoy some quiet reflection.

3. Try Something New

Learning a new skill—painting, cooking, or coding—stimulates your brain and encourages creative problem solving.
